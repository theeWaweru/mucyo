My Story
About me

African artist and photographer born in South Africa, made of Rwanda, from Nairobi, Kenya.

Gasana's photography explores the beauty and truth of daily life and everyday people. By choosing the precise instance between the subject and the moment he hopes to share his vision by transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary and the real into the surreal.

He primarily shoots on 35mm and medium format film which he develops and scans himself.
